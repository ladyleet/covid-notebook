### Lemongrass Pork Skewers

Recipe:
1-1.5 lb pork butt
2 tbs fish sauce
2 tbs oyster sauce
2 tbs sweet soy sauce
2 tbs sugar
6 cloves garlic minced
2 stalks lemongrass minced
1 tbs oil
1 tsp black pepper

Marinate overnight for best flavor or at least 4hrs. Cook 18mins 375
